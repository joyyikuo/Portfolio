Welcome to my analytics portfolio! This portfolio site was built to mimic an extremely simplified version of the Pirate Metrics, through the tracking of acquisition, activation, retention, referral, revenue via Google Analytics.

  • Acquisition tracks the number of visits this portfolio site gets by active sessions.
  • Activation tracks inital engagement, which looks at the number of sessions that clicks on a button or link on the site.
  • Retention tracks visitors that return to the site. As this demo tracks sessions instead of users, all visits (sessions) made by returning user will be tracked.
  • Referral typically tracks whether or not the site is being recommended by visitors. As sharing options are not included, the number of sessions that are from referred sources, meaning access that are not from direct link input, will be shown instead.
  • Revenue tracks purchases. On this portfolio site, whenever a visitor clicks on the money button, a $1 revenue is taken into account.
